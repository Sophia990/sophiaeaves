Mighty Morphin Power Rangers: Rita's Rewind

Mighty Morphin Power Rangers: Rita's Rewind is a 2D brawler with an original Power Rangers storyline, which I worked on during my internship at Digital Eclipse. I developed a level for the game, using Lua and C++, and was given the opportunity to contribute ideas and suggestions.

See more

My Contributions

Although my level has been reworked somewhat since the end of my internship, I helped to develop it from being just a road with coins, to having enemies, obstacles, and an environment around it.

I made choices about where to introduce the enemies and obstacles, added the buildings and trees, and ensured that no more buildings, trees or road than aboslutely necessary were present to optimise the level.

To introduce these elements to the level, I used the scripting language Lua, which was the first time I had used only a scripting language like this to add objects to a game. I quickly realised that I should be using constants and adding to the previous object's constant to decide distances between objects, to save needing to fiddle with lots of numbers.

Moreover, I used C++ to change the behaviour of various enemies and obstacles, and to create a new obstacle, although these additions did not make it into the final game. This was my first experience of C++ code used in a professional game, and I was able to develop my knowledge of some programming concepts used in C++, such as bitwise operations.
